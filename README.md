# 📖 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the **most critical problems in subscription businesses**.  
Losing customers directly impacts **revenue, growth, and customer acquisition costs**.

This project builds a **churn prediction system** that combines:

• Machine Learning prediction  
• Risk segmentation  
• Business intelligence dashboards  

The goal is to identify **customers most likely to churn** and surface **actionable insights** for retention strategies.

The project is divided into **two dashboards**:

| Dashboard | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Business Analysis Dashboard** | Understand historical churn behavior |
| **ML Risk Intelligence Dashboard** | Predict future churn risk |

---

# 🧠 Churn Prediction Pipeline

The project follows a **machine learning analytics workflow**:

```
Raw Telecom Dataset
        ↓
Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering (Python / Pandas)
        ↓
Machine Learning Classification Model
        ↓
Customer Churn Probability Scores
        ↓
Risk Segmentation (Risk Buckets + Deciles)
        ↓
Interactive Tableau Dashboards
```

This pipeline transforms raw customer data into **actionable churn risk insights**.

---

# 📊 Dashboard 1 — Business Analysis

This dashboard explores **historical churn patterns**.

It helps answer questions such as:

• Which customers churn most often?  
• Which services increase churn risk?  
• How does contract length impact retention?

### Key Metrics

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Customers | Total customer base analyzed |
| Churn Rate | Percentage of customers who churned |
| Avg Monthly Charges | Average monthly customer revenue |
| High Risk Customers | Customers predicted to churn |

### Visualizations

• Churn by Contract Type  
• Churn by Internet Service  
• Churn by Tenure  
• Interactive filtering across customer attributes

These insights help identify **behavioral drivers of churn**.

---

# 🤖 Dashboard 2 — ML Risk Intelligence

The second dashboard focuses on **predictive churn analysis**.

Instead of analyzing past churn, it answers:

> **Which customers are likely to churn next?**

### Model KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Avg Churn Probability | Average predicted churn likelihood |
| High Risk Customers | Customers with churn probability ≥ 60% |

---

### ML Model Insights

#### 📈 Churn Probability Distribution
Shows how the machine learning model distributes customers across churn probabilities.

Helps identify:

• clusters of high-risk customers  
• confidence levels of the model  

---

#### 🔢 Risk Decile Breakdown
Customers are ranked into **10 deciles based on churn probability**.

Decile 10 contains the **highest-risk customers**.

This segmentation helps prioritize **retention campaigns**.

---

### Business Action Insights

#### ⚠️ High Risk Customers by Contract Type

The model reveals a clear pattern:

| Contract Type | High Risk Customers |
|---|---|
| Month-to-month | Highest risk |
| One year | Moderate risk |
| Two year | Very low risk |

This aligns with industry research that **longer contracts reduce churn risk**.

---

#### 💰 Retention Opportunity by Risk Segment

This visualization translates ML predictions into **revenue impact**.

It estimates **monthly revenue at risk** from churn across segments:

| Risk Segment | Revenue Exposure |
|---|---|
| High Risk | Immediate retention priority |
| Medium Risk | Preventative engagement |
| Low Risk | Stable customer base |

This allows businesses to **focus retention resources where they matter most**.
